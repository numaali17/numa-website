Welcome to My Website

Hello! My name is Numa Ali, and I am a Business Administration student at Northeastern University studying Finance and International Business with a minor in Data Science. I am passionate about learning about the world, different cultures, and how global connections shape the way people live and work. One of the things that inspires this curiosity is traveling. Traveling allows me to explore new places, meet people from different backgrounds, and experience cultures that are different from my own. For me, traveling is more than just visiting new destinations. It is about understanding history, traditions, and perspectives from around the world. Each place has its own unique story, whether it is reflected in the architecture, food, language, or daily life of the people who live there. Traveling helps me become more open-minded and gives me a broader understanding of how interconnected our world really is. As someone studying international business, traveling also connects closely with my academic interests. Seeing different countries and markets helps me understand how global economies work and how culture influences business and communication. I enjoy learning how businesses operate in different parts of the world and how innovation and technology are shaping global industries. Through this website, I want to share my passion for traveling and some of the destinations that inspire me the most. Exploring new places motivates me to keep learning, growing, and connecting with the world around me.
